# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 16, 2026

Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how AuraLens collects, uses, and protects your information.

**1. Data Collection**
AuraLens is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. All core processing happens locally on your device. 

**2. Permissions**
If AuraLens requests access to your Camera or Photo Library, it is strictly to provide the app's core functionality. We do not upload your images or videos to any external servers.

**3. Changes to This Policy**
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

**4. Contact Us**
For any privacy-related questions or concerns, please reach out to us using the contact information provided above.
