## 🛠️ Support

### Frequently Asked Questions

> **What does AuraLens do?**
> AuraLens analyzes your skin using AI technology and provides personalized skincare suggestions based on your skin type, concerns, and goals.

> **Is AuraLens a medical app?**
> No. AuraLens is a wellness and self-care tool. It does not diagnose medical conditions or replace professional dermatological advice. If you have skin concerns, please consult a healthcare professional.

> **Are my photos stored or shared?**
> Your photos are stored only on your device. When you request an analysis, the photo is sent to our AI provider for processing but is not retained afterward. We never share your photos with third parties.

> **How do I delete my data?**
> All data is stored locally on your device. To delete everything, simply delete the AuraLens app from your device. All photos, analysis history, and preferences will be permanently removed.

> **The analysis seems inaccurate. What should I do?**
> For best results, take photos in good natural lighting with your face clearly visible. Avoid heavy makeup or filters. If results still seem off, try retaking the photo.

> **I'm having technical issues.**
> Try the following: (1) Close and reopen the app. (2) Make sure you have a stable internet connection. (3) Update to the latest version of AuraLens. (4) Restart your device.

> **How do I cancel my subscription?**
> Go to your iPhone's **Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → AuraLens → Cancel Subscription**. You will retain access until the end of your current billing period.

## 🔒 Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 19, 2026

### 1. Introduction

AuraLens ("we," "our," or "the app") is a skincare analysis application that provides general wellness suggestions. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

### 2. Information We Collect

| Data Type | Details | Stored Where |
|-----------|---------|--------------|
| 📸 **Photos** | Camera and photo library access for skin analysis only. Processed in real-time. | On your device only |
| 👤 **User Profile** | Age, skin type, and skin concerns from onboarding. | On your device only |
| 📊 **Usage Data** | We do **not** collect analytics, tracking data, or usage statistics. | N/A |

### 3. Third-Party AI Services

AuraLens uses **Anthropic's Claude AI** service to analyze your skin photos and provide personalized skincare recommendations.

| | Details |
|---|---|
| 📤 **What is sent** | Your facial photo (compressed image data only). No name, email, age, or other personal identifiers are sent. |
| 🏢 **Who it is sent to** | Anthropic, PBC ([anthropic.com](https://www.anthropic.com)) — the provider of the Claude AI model |
| 🎯 **Why it is sent** | To perform AI-powered skin condition analysis, generate personalized skincare tasks, and compare progress photos during the 7-Day Glow Challenge |
| 🔐 **How it is processed** | Transmitted via encrypted HTTPS connection. Analyzed in real-time and **not stored on Anthropic's servers** after processing. Anthropic processes data per their [privacy policy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy). |
| ✅ **Your permission** | The app shows a clear consent dialog **before** any photo is sent for the first time. You must tap "I Agree" before any data is transmitted. If you decline, no data is sent. |

### 4. Data Storage

All personal data, including photos, analysis results, and preferences, is stored **locally on your device**. We do not maintain any external database or server that stores your personal information.

### 5. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. The only data transmission occurs when you explicitly request an AI skin analysis, as described in Section 3.

### 6. Children's Privacy

AuraLens is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

### 7. Medical Disclaimer

⚠️ AuraLens is **not** a medical device and does not provide medical diagnoses, treatment advice, or professional dermatological consultations. The analysis and suggestions are for general informational and wellness purposes only. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical concerns.
