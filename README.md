  #   Support Page

  **Welcome to AuraLens Support**

  **Q: What does AuraLens do?**
  AuraLens analyzes your skin using AI technology and provides personalized skincare suggestions based on your skin type, concerns, and
  goals.

  **Q: Is AuraLens a medical app?**
  No. AuraLens is a wellness and self-care tool. It does not diagnose medical conditions or replace professional dermatological advice. If
  you have skin concerns, please consult a healthcare professional.

  **Q: Are my photos stored or shared?**
  Your photos are stored only on your device. When you request an analysis, the photo is sent to our AI provider for processing but is not
  retained afterward. We never share your photos with third parties.

  **Q: How do I delete my data?**
  All data is stored locally on your device. To delete everything, simply delete the AuraLens app from your device. All photos, analysis
  history, and preferences will be permanently removed.

  **Q: The analysis seems inaccurate. What should I do?**
  For best results, take photos in good natural lighting with your face clearly visible. Avoid heavy makeup or filters. If results still
  seem off, try retaking the photo.

  **Q: I'm having technical issues.**
  Try the following steps:
  1. Close and reopen the app.
  2. Make sure you have a stable internet connection (required for AI analysis).
  3. Update to the latest version of AuraLens.
  4. Restart your device.

  **Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**
  Go to your iPhone's **Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > AuraLens > Cancel Subscription**. You will retain access until the end of
  your current billing period.

  **Contact Us:**
  For any questions, issues, or feedback, reach us at: **auralenscare@gmail.com**

  ## Privacy Policy

  **Last Updated:** March 19, 2026

  **1. Introduction**
  AuraLens ("we," "our," or "the app") is a skincare analysis application that provides general wellness suggestions. This Privacy Policy
  explains how we handle your information.

  **2. Information We Collect**
  - **Photos:** We access your camera and photo library solely to perform skin analysis. Photos are processed in real-time and are stored
  only on your device. We do not upload or store your photos on any external server.
  - **User Profile Data:** Age, skin type, and skin concerns you provide during onboarding are stored locally on your device to personalize
   your experience.
  - **Usage Data:** We do not collect analytics, tracking data, or usage statistics.

  **3. Third-Party AI Services**
  AuraLens uses **Anthropic's Claude AI** service to analyze your skin photos and provide personalized skincare recommendations.

  **What data is sent:**
  - Your facial photo (compressed image data only)
  - No name, email, age, or other personal identifiers are sent to the AI service

  **Who it is sent to:**
  - Anthropic, PBC (https://www.anthropic.com) — the provider of the Claude AI model

  **Why it is sent:**
  - To perform AI-powered skin condition analysis, generate personalized skincare task recommendations, and compare progress photos during
  the 7-Day Glow Challenge

  **How it is processed:**
  - Your photo is transmitted via an encrypted HTTPS connection
  - The photo is analyzed in real-time and is **not stored on Anthropic's servers** after processing
  - Anthropic processes the data in accordance with their own privacy policy (https://www.anthropic.com/privacy)

  **Your permission:**
  - The app displays a clear consent dialog **before** any photo is sent to the AI service for the first time
  - You must explicitly tap "I Agree" before any data is transmitted
  - If you decline, no data is sent and the analysis feature will not be available

  **4. Data Storage**
  All personal data, including photos, analysis results, and preferences, is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain any external
   database or server that stores your personal information.

  **5. Data Sharing**
  We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. The only data
  transmission occurs when you explicitly request an AI skin analysis, as described in Section 3 above.

  **6. Children's Privacy**
  AuraLens is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

  **7. Medical Disclaimer**
  AuraLens is not a medical device and does not provide medical diagnoses, treatment advice, or professional dermatological consultations.
  The analysis and suggestions provided are for general informational and wellness purposes only. Always consult a qualified healthcare
  professional for medical concerns.

  **8. Subscriptions**
  AuraLens offers auto-renewable subscriptions (Weekly Premium and Yearly Premium) that provide unlimited photo analysis and full access to
   the 7-Day Glow Challenge. Subscription automatically renews unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the
  current period. Payment will be charged to your Apple ID account. You can manage and cancel subscriptions in your device's Settings >
  Apple ID > Subscriptions.

  **9. Changes to This Policy**
  We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.

  **10. Your Consent**
  By using AuraLens and agreeing to the in-app consent dialog, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in
  this Privacy Policy, including the transmission of your photos to Anthropic's Claude AI service for skin analysis purposes.

  **11. Contact Us**
  If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: **auralenscare@gmail.com**
