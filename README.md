## 🛠️ Support Page

**Welcome to AuraLens Support**

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What does AuraLens do?** **A:** AuraLens analyzes your skin using AI technology and provides personalized skincare suggestions based on your skin type, concerns, and goals.

**Q: Is AuraLens a medical app?** **A:** No. AuraLens is a wellness and self-care tool. It does not diagnose medical conditions or replace professional dermatological advice. If you have skin concerns, please consult a healthcare professional.

**Q: Are my photos stored or shared?** **A:** Your photos are stored only on your device. When you request an analysis, the photo is sent to our AI provider for processing but is not retained afterward. We never share your photos with third parties.

**Q: How do I delete my data?** **A:** All data is stored locally on your device. To delete everything, simply delete the AuraLens app from your device. All photos, analysis history, and preferences will be permanently removed.

**Q: The analysis seems inaccurate. What should I do?** **A:** For best results, take photos in good natural lighting with your face clearly visible. Avoid heavy makeup or filters. If results still seem off, try retaking the photo.

**Q: I'm having technical issues.** **A:** Try the following steps:
1. Close and reopen the app.
2. Make sure you have a stable internet connection (required for AI analysis).
3. Update to the latest version of AuraLens.
4. Restart your device.

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?** **A:** Go to your iPhone's **Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → AuraLens → Cancel Subscription**. You will retain access until the end of your current billing period.

## 🔒 Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 16, 2026

### 1. Introduction
AuraLens ("we," "our," or "the app") is a skincare analysis application that provides general wellness suggestions. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

### 2. Information We Collect
* **Photos:** We access your camera and photo library solely to perform skin analysis. Photos are processed in real-time and are stored only on your device. We do not upload, store, or share your photos on any server.
* **User Profile Data:** Age, skin type, and skin concerns you provide during onboarding are stored locally on your device to personalize your experience.
* **Usage Data:** We do not collect analytics, tracking data, or usage statistics.

### 3. Third-Party Services
We use third-party AI services (OpenAI, Anthropic Claude) to analyze skin photos. When you request an analysis, your photo is sent to these services for processing. These services process the data in accordance with their own privacy policies and do not retain your images after processing.

### 4. Data Storage
All personal data, including photos, analysis results, and preferences, is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain any external database or server that stores your personal information.

### 5. Data Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

### 6. Children's Privacy
AuraLens is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

### 7. Medical Disclaimer
AuraLens is not a medical device and does not provide medical diagnoses, treatment advice, or professional dermatological consultations. The analysis and suggestions provided are for general informational and wellness purposes only. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical concerns.

### 8. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
